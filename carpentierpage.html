I am a Simons Postdoctoral Research Scientist in the mathematics department of Columbia University. I received my PhD from MIT in 2017, under the supervision of Victor Kac. You can find more details in my CV.

Research

I am mostly interested in Mathematical Physics. More precisely, my research focuses on integrable systems of PDEs and of differential-difference equations.
